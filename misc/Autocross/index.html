Autocross

Navigating Cones in my 2023 Civic Si

First Event: 5/25/25

I've always been a fan of racing. Whether it be watching F1, playing racing games, whatever, I've always wished I could too. Enter Autocross, a form of racing that is accessible to anyone with a car and a helmet. Last year, my Dad had bought an orange 2023 Honda Civic Si. A manual. A car that is fun to drive, and a car that can go decently fast. But mainly, a car that can be driven in Autocross. Autocross is a form of racing where drivers are racing against the clock on a course in a large parking lot. Drivers have to navigate through a series of cones, trying to get the fastest time possible. Its fairly simple, easy to learn, cheap, and most importantly, fun. I recommend anyone interested in racing to try it out.
