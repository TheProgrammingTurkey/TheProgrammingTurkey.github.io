Ping

Top Down Ping Pong Game

6/16/23

Ping was my take on the classic "Pong" game made by Atari. Because it was my first JS canvas project, it took a lot of learning and getting used to. This caused it to take a longer time to make than I thought, taking just about five months during the second semester of my freshman year of highschool. I modified the original game by adding powerups and making the paddles close in towards each other. With three different gamemodes and three difficulties, Ping is worth a try.
