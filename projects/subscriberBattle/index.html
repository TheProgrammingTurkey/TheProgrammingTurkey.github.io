Subscriber Battle

Youtube Statistic Tracker Between 2 Channels

3/7/25

Two of my friends have been in a little youtube subscriber battle for a little while, and I thought it would be fun to make a website to track both of their websites. Instead of going to each of their channels every time they wanted to check, they could just go to this website and see the current subscriber count of both of them. It also shows their views and views per video. There's really nothing else to it, but it was a fun little project to make. Anyone wishing to make something similar can always fork my repository and change the channel ids to their own.
