Looping Wranglers

GTMK 2025: Loop

8/3/25

Looping Wranglers was my submission for the 2025 Game Makers Toolkit Game Jam. The theme was "Loop," so I created a western themed game with a lasso to fit the theme. Being my first every game jam I've completed, I'm just happy that I was able to successfully submit a full complete game in time. In a nutshell, the game works with you controlling the cowboy, can you have to spin around in a circle and throw your lasso to wrangle all of your cattle back to their pen. The game is quite simple and straightforward but still fairly fun. Give it a try, but remember it was made in roughly 10 hours.

What I learned: Nothing :(
